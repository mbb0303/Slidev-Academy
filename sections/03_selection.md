# Grouped selection criteria

::left::

## Charged tracks

- $N_c\ge2$
- $|\cos\theta|<0.93$
- $V_r<0.5~\mathrm{cm},\ |V_z|<5~\mathrm{cm}$

## Particle identification

- $P_{\mathrm{PID}}>10^{-3}$
- Choose the candidate with the largest combined probability.

::right::

## Signal region

- $0.92<M_{\mathrm{recoil}}<0.96~\mathrm{GeV}/c^2$
- Sidebands are kept for validation.
